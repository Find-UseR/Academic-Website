About BIIT

Barani Institute of Information Technology (BIIT)

Barani Institute of Information Technology (BIIT) is a renowned educational institution located in Pakistan. It is known for its exceptional quality of education and its commitment to producing skilled professionals in the field of information technology. BIIT is affiliated with the Pir Mehr Ali Shah Arid Agriculture University, Rawalpindi, and offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in computer science and information technology. BIIT was established at University of Arid Agriculture after a long and deliberate consideration of the growing needs of software development and the initiative launched by Government of Pakistan. The institute was established as a partnership venture between the University of Arid Agriculture (UAAR) and M/S Resource Organizers and Software Engineers (ROSE) International.
